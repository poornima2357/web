About School

EAB Govt Higher Secondary School is committed to providing quality education and shaping the future of young minds. The school focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and the overall development of students through a balanced approach to learning. With well-equipped facilities such as a rich library, modern laboratories, and sports infrastructure, the institution supports both intellectual and physical growth. Our dedicated teachers strive to create a positive and inspiring learning environment that encourages creativity, confidence, and leadership. The mission of the school is to develop responsible citizens with strong values and a lifelong passion for learning.
